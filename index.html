02. About

Design that works
for people who
can’t afford friction

I’m Jordi Moca, a Product Designer with 7+ years designing interfaces that experts depend on — doctors prescribing medication, journalists publishing content, bankers managing transactions.

My work is grounded in one conviction: good design is invisible. The best interface gets out of the way and lets the user do their job. That means obsessing over hierarchy, reducing cognitive load, and respecting how real people work under pressure.

Based in Barcelona, Catalonia. Available for any challenging design process.
